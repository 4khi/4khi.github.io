Increasing one's fitness for the
sake of الله

 

One must understand that the mujahid should be ready at all times for war so that if a khilafah is established and the khalifah calls upon him to fight for the sake of الله against the kuffar in order to implement the word of الله he should be combat ready so that he can be capable in the battlefield and so that he should not be a burden upon his brothers.

If one is not at the required capacity, he should work towards the goal of attaining this state of physical and mental strength as well as that of his iman , with sincere intentions of earning the pleasure of

سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَى ‎ الله

 

Increasing mental strength:

1) Take cold showers

2) Do not masturbate or watch porn

3) Have Taqwa

4) Try to travel by walk or bike (if it is safe to do so in your are) to the masjid for all your prayer. Travelling by walk/bike at night/early morning has benefit of decreasing your innate fear of the dark.

5) Lower your gaze.

6) Fast for the sake of pleasing Allah. Also has additional benefit of increasing mental strength

 

 

Methods of increasing physical strength:

1) Walk/Run/Bike to masjid for all congregational prayers whilst reciting dhikr/quran

2) Do Pull Ups whilst reciting dhikr/quran

3) Do Push Ups whilst reciting dhikr/quran

4) Lift heavy things whilst reciting dhikr/quran
